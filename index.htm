Experience the scenic beauty and serenity of the Texas Hill Country at Dripping Springs' most tranquil and secluded bed and breakfast accommodation where hospitality is foremost.
Tex-Zen Dreams Bed and Breakfast is a honeymoon and anniversary retreat, an artist / writer retreat, a weekend getaway place, and a tranquil place to accommodate visiting friends and relatives, wedding guests, and hill country travelers.
Tex-Zen Dreams also features multiple outdoor event venues, and an on-site wedding planner & event coordinator.
