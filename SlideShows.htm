Rose Garden Slide Shows

Before starting construction we found an eight foot metal octagonal garden gazebo - a pefect arbor for the centerpiece of the rose garden.

It was rusty and Loretta didn't want a big job of rust removal so the first thing we did was hasten the process with an application of phosphoric acid. The rust color actually complements the cinnamon theme color of the bridge and gazebo. Then we applied a couple coats of clear enamel just to keep the orange-colored rust off us until the climbing roses could cover the arbor.
