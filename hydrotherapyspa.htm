Tex-Zen Dreams Hydrotherapy Spa / Salt Water Swimming Pool

 

An alluring oasis on the hillside that merges Texas hospitality with sunsets over the creek valley, nighttime stargazing, and cooing of doves from the nearby aviary, also provides the perfect entertainment and relaxation ambiance to enhance your dream getaway weekend. Enjoy the charm of warm summer nights as you and other guests gather on our furnished deck for fun, conversation, or stargazing.  Let the Southern appeal of Tex-Zen Dreams hydrotherapy spa and salt water swimming pool create either a warming or a cooling retreat from the stress and demands of everyday life.

 

Comfortably seating 5-6, the hydrotherapy spa features conformed, bench, and lounge seating to relieve stress and calm the muscles. Jet placement in the lounger seat provides an invigorating massage to back, legs, and feet. Contoured seating also features back, leg, and neck jets. Two 5hp pumps driving forty-five (45) jets in five (5) varieties enliven 420 gallons of heated water, creating a tingling oasis of hydrothermal therapy. LED waterline lighting and dramatic light settings create the perfect mood for fun, romance, or serenity.

 

The table-top gas fire pit adds an air of enchantment to a late Fall or early winter evening with an open display of glowing fire. Gather around for early-evening merriment with Cottage-of-Dreams guests or with the innkeepers. Relax to the serenity of a glowing fire in the still night. The fire pit is sure to cast welcoming warmth over the outdoor experience. Nothing compares to the enchanting glow of an outdoor fire, adding a sense of magic, welcome, and comfort to the spa and pool deck. Enjoy the simple pleasures of roasting marshmallows and hot dogs, or gather around for a lively night of ghost stories as the moon makes its ascent into the starry night or its descent into the darkness of the valley. The table-top fire pit emanates the perfect ambiance for sophisticated or casual entertainment.
