RAIN CELEBRATION!

Dear guests and friends,

Many of you expressed the desire to come back when the creek was flowing so that you could experience all the natural beauty and tranquility that our little paradise here in the hill country has to offer. Through either prayer or dance, the 6.5" March rains have replenished our rainwater storage tanks and saturated the hillsides again. The springs are dripping again to return our creek-side setting to its most Zensational splendor.

In appreciation for your patronage we are offering former guests who stayed with us from May 23, 2005 to March 26, 2007 a 10% discount on our current multi-night rate during your next stay with us while the creek is still flowing. That's $18 off the weekend and $16 off the weekday two-night rate. Come visit us again before the hillsides and creek dry up again for the summer. Come enjoy the views and ambiance of our hydrotherapy DreamSpa and gazebo opened January 2006 next to the Cottage-of-Dreams. Call, e-mail, or check room availability and rates on our B & B page. You can now check the creek status on our Ranch Tour page.

We want to truly thank all you folks for sharing your kind comments in our guest books. Thanks for staying with us during the first year of the B & B and over the past year. We look forward to seeing you at Tex-Zen Dreams B & B again soon before the creek goes dry for summer.
