Spring/Fall 2011 Wedding/Event Packages / Coordination Services

Every Event A Dream Come True


I - Petite Dream Wedding/Event

  • Scenic 20ft arched bridge crossing a seasonal branch of Sycamore creek.
  • 14’ x 20’ gazebo lighted with white ceiling fans.
  • Rose garden complete with vine-covered arbor.
  • Stone dam creating a seasonal waterfall.
  • The Mystical "Marrying Rock" completely surrounded by water.
  • Appropriate decorative enhancements for the bridge and gazebo including colored ribbon, white tulle, and greenery.

This package includes the use of the "Park Grounds" for the event and includes an additional two hour rehearsal/planning session to be scheduled during the previous week. Also includes one restroom facility.

Base price1: $450.00/ 1st hr.; $100.00/ ea. additional hr. between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 10:00 p.m.

Dressing rooms at Bouquet & Boutonniere cottage, if available, additional $75.00/rm

II - Petite Dream Wedding/Event and Reception

Includes everything in the Petite Dream Wedding/Event plus:

  • Dressing rooms at Bouquet & Boutonniere cottage.
  • 3 serving tables incl. linens.

  • Seating for 19 guests. Chairs in the front row of the gazebo are clothed in white and tied with appropriate colored ribbon.

This package includes the day-use of the B & B Cottage for the contracted time period and for the bride and groom, bridesmaids and groomsmen (or two couples) only - not for guest use. Cottage-of-Dreams "House Rules" apply (except for check-in/out rules).

Base Price1: $600.00/ 1st hr.; $100/ ea. additional hr. between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 10:00 p.m.

III - Amazing Dream Wedding/Event and Reception

Includes everything in the Petite Dream Wedding/Event and Reception but allows you to express your limitless dreams with the aid of our certified wedding/event coordinator.

We start with an overall theme and personalize it with embellishments to orchestrate your own one-of-a-kind event. Let us help to create your hearts' desire. If you can dream it we can do it! We encourage the bride and groom along with family and friends to sit back, relax and enjoy the most beautifully stress-free and memorable day a couple can share as they begin a new life together. Come enjoy the gracious hospitality of TEX-ZEN DREAMS and your professional wedding specialist as we help you realize your own Amazing Dream. Coordination fees can be negotiated hourly or as a percentage of your total event expenditure/budget.

Note: Many services can be brought to you when you stay over night in one of our beautifully appointed guest rooms.
