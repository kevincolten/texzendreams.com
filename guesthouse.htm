Relax-Zen Guest House - Group/Vacation Home

Formerly our full-time Hillview Guest House, the Relax-Zen Guest House is now available for nightly accommodations. The newly refurbished and fully furnished 1350 sq.ft., 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom Relax-Zen Guest House accommodates up to six or seven guests, including children. The home features a hill country landscape, open kitchen-dining-living floorplan, central air & heat, ceiling fans, plenty of storage cabinetry, spacious bedrooms, natural lighting, and laundry room. The kitchen boasts a gas range oven, large refrig/freezer with icemaker, dishwasher, and countertop microwave, blender, coffee maker, and comes fully stocked with dishware, cookware and utensils. The master suite is complete with a large walk-in closet, king size dual adjustable power bed, a large relaxing chair as well as a cozy bistro seating area. The en-suite bathroom features double sinks, shower stall, and a large corner soaking tub. Both bathroooms are fully stocked with towels, linens and soaps. The middle bedroom features a full size bed and warm, cozy decor. The end bedroom features a queen bed, bay window, dvd/tv, and bright and colorful decor. The living area features a leather reclining/rocker, dual comfy living room chairs, and a large comfy sofa for seating while watching DVD movies or satelite programming on the amazing 55" 4K HDTV. The dining room table features built-in extensions for seating six and there is seating for two or three at the kitchen bar. Outside front and back you will find a lovely treed yard area suitable for entertaining. 2 car parking available in both front and rear. The house is nestled just 100 ft from the main residence on a gently sloping 10+ acre hillside overlooking Sycamore Creek, a creekside gazebo and a unique arched foot bridge. Located minutes away from multiple event venues, winery, olive orchard, and State Park! Come for a relax-zen weekend getaway or vacation now! See virtual tour.

The on-site B & B innkeepers and Guest House proprietors, Loretta and Jeff live in the residence on the property, are dedicated to hospitality, and are enthusiastically available for help with their guest's hill country experience or event planning.

Ambiance

The endeavor at Tex-Zen Dreams Relax-Zen Guest House is to provide an oasis of peace and tranquility from the everyday world; consequently we provide no land-line telephones and no wi-fi service. The Guest House accommodates a maximum of seven, and to preserve the high quality of the indoor environment, no smoking and no pets are allowed. It's your choice to mingle with the innkeepers at the main residence, pool, creek-side gazebo, and/or enjoy your privacy at the Guest House.

Camp Zen - Alternative "Glam Camping" Experience

Tex-Zen Dreams also offers an alternative "glam camping" experience in a 30 ft. Holiday Rambler travel trailer parked at a hillside campsite between the rainwater barn and the newly refurbished Hillview Guest/Vacation House. Camp Zen accommodates up to four or five guests, including children, and a pet. Camp Zen is currently booked full time. See Camp Zen details.

Location

By road, Tex-Zen Dreams is centrally located 24 miles north of Wimberley, 18 miles east of Johnson City, about 25 miles south of Lake Travis, and about 29 miles west of downtown Austin. Tex-Zen Dreams is 9 miles northwest of central Dripping Springs and only 7 miles east of Pedernales Falls State Park. Visible from the property, the site of the annual Austin Muddy Buddy Ride and Run as well as the Mellow Johnny's Classic mountain bike race at Flat Creek Crossing is a short 5 mile, 10 minute ride away.

Be sure to check our map page for location and directions. PLEASE don't depend on your GPSr or vehicle navigation system to lead you here unless you are sure your maps are updated. OLDER MAPS WILL LEAD YOU IN THE WRONG DIRECTION AFTER YOU GET WITHIN 2 MILES OF HERE.

Area Things To Do and Nearby Destinations

Come enjoy the peaceful serenity of a romantic Hill Country weekend retreat with the one you love. Tour one of the nearby wineries, micro breweries, or the Texas Hill Country Olive Company ; shop for art or antiques in Wimberley; hike Pedernales Falls, Hamilton pool, or Westcave Preserve; bike the country back roads; relax listening to the sounds of falling water with a picnic at our creek-side gazebo; soak in the hydrotherapy spa in the winter, cool off in the salt-water swimming pool in the summer, or nap in the comfy hammock just outside your door.
