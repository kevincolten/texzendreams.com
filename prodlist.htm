HOME:

In 1997 we began harvesting the wild mustang grapes growing on our ranch and rainwater collected on our 3200 sq.ft. rainbarn to produce home-made jelly and wine gifts for our visiting friends and relatives. In keeping with winemaking tradition, we home-labeled both with a vintage date representing the year of harvest.

Our friends loved the labels so that project quickly expanded to the production of our 1997 vintage Pear Butter made from Kieffer pears we rescued from some trees in Johnson City before the frost could take them. Then our "654321 Salsa" recipe provided by a good friend followed. Our first 1997 vintage "Tex-Zen Trio™" gift box was so well received that we started using other fruit varieties to keep up with the requests. By just the second season, Tex-Zen Dreams was offering to our guests 15 varieties of jams, jellies, preserves, butters and salsa, all hand-picked, home-prepared with all natural ingredients . . . including naturally pure rainwater.

If you would like to use your credit card to purchase some of our home-made jams, jellies, preserves, butters or salsas, please use our on-line shopping cart hosted by PayPal - it's fast, free and secure! If you aren't in a hurry and want to make payment by sending check or money order, please try our order form. If you have problems or need help, e-mail us or use our "Contact Us" form. Please note that our on-line store shipments can only be made to destinations within the continental U.S.
