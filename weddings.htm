We are an outdoor-only wedding and event venue, but unlike other wedding service facilities, our venues are not miniature replicas or plastic props. To the contrary we have:

   Multiple Venues

  • Tranquil Creek-side Setting - Stately oaks and elms with both sides of Sycamore Creek
  • Gazebo - A 14 ft x 20 ft wooden oval with lighted ceiling fans
  • Arched Bridge - A lighted 20 foot long steel and wood arched bridge across the creek
  • The ''Marrying Rock'' - Texas' only "Marrying Rock", a prominent natural formation that is completely surrounded by Sycamore Creek
  • Waterfall and Relection Pool - A 50 foot long natural stone and concrete dam promotes creek water retention to create the seasonal reflection pool and waterfall
  • Rose Garden - A quarter acre garden complete with eight foot vine and rose covered ornamental arbor. 75+ rosebushes.
  • Wild Grape Vineyard - A quarter acre grape-producing vineyard

Winter scene

Tex-Zen Gazebo is large enough for the wedding participants on one side, plus up to 19 seated guests on the other. An intimate ceremony-in-the-round can be accomodated with a small wedding party in the center and up to 24 seated guests on both sides. For ceremonies with more attendants, use the entire gazebo for the wedding party. Surrounding tiered seating under the trees can comfortably accomodate 100 guests and more with additional surrounding seating and standing areas.


Tex-Zen Dreams offers a lighted 20-ft arched bridge for your wedding processional and the only known balanced wedding rock in the Hill Country completely surrounded by water for a most unique wedding ceremony.
