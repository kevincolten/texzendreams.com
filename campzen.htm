Camp Zen

Experience glam camping while enjoying the scenic beauty and serenity of the rural Texas Hill Country at Tex-Zen Dream's Camp Zen. Nestled 250 ft from the main residence on a hillside directly adjacent to the rainwater storage barn, Camp Zen offers a 30 ft. Holiday Rambler travel trailer with two slide-out rooms creating approx. 250sqft living space. The space is immaculately clean and well-appointed, includes air conditioning, electric fireplace, bathroom with shower, separate bedroom with queen memory-foam bed, dining booth, kitchenette, living area with sofa, two televisions, am/fm stereo, deluxe double pleated window shades, and copius lighting. Bedding is provided for converting the dinette and sofa into twin beds.

Ambiance -

The endeavor at Tex-Zen Dreams B & B is to provide an oasis of peace and tranquility from the everyday world. Consequently we provide no land-line telephones and no wi-fi service. Camp Zen accommodates up to four adults or five guests if some are small children. To preserve the quality of the indoor environment, no smoking is allowed. It's your choice to mingle with the innkeepers at the main residence, pool, and/or creekside gazebo, or enjoy your privacy at your campsite.
