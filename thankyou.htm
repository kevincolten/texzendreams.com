“A Dream Day”

       I wanted the perfect wedding day, and spent a lot of time, energy, creativity and deliberation in planning every single detail.  It took a year to get it just right, a perfect reflection of us, and then it’s here….W-Day.   Surely everything would go off without a hitch, perfectly smooth right?  I’ve heard horror stories of weddings gone wrong and the realist that I am, I was fully prepared for a few hiccups here and there.  This is after all the real world.  But what I hadn’t fully realized was what Loretta would do behind the scenes.  She certainly helped in planning the wedding, but I took great enjoyment in doing a lot of the preparation.  What I wasn’t looking forward to was orchestrating the day as it unfolded.  And I didn’t have to.  Loretta was the first person there and kept the day on track and just as planned.  I’m sure issues came up, they always do, but I don’t know what it was or how it was handled.  All I know is that the day went EXACTLY as planned and all we had to do was show up and enjoy our day surrounded by family and friends. 

Thank you is not enough!

Demetra and Eric Salting
